# Student Life Co-Pilot

A production-ready, institution-grade student success and wellbeing platform designed for top-tier universities. This application integrates Academics, Mental Wellbeing, Career Planning, and Technical Skills into a unified ecosystem.

### For Students
*   **Unified Dashboard**: Academic health, upcoming deadlines, and wellbeing status at a glance.
*   **AI Wellbeing Coach**: Daily mood check-ins, sleep tracking, and AI-powered journaling support.
*   **Career Co-Pilot**: Skill gap analysis, personalized roadmaps, and AI resume tips.
*   **Gamified Learning**: DSA practice with XP, streaks, and badges.
*   **Finance Manager**: Educational budget tracking and expense categorization.

### For Faculty
*   **Risk Monitor**: AI-driven early warning system for at-risk students (based on attendance & grades).
*   **Engagement Analytics**: Class-level trends and engagement scores.
*   **Anonymous Support Box**: Privacy-first channel for student concerns.
*   **Intervention Tools**: One-click email or meeting scheduling for flagged students.

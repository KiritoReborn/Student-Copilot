psychology How Our AI Works

Algorithmic Auditing Process expand_more
Our models undergo rigorous quarterly audits by third-party ethics boards. We specifically test for demographic parity to ensure no student group is unfairly penalized by automated flags. The last audit was completed on September 15, 2023, passing with a 98% fairness score.
Human-in-the-Loop Validation expand_more
AI never makes final decisions on academic standing. All "High Risk" alerts generated by the system must be reviewed and validated by a faculty member or academic advisor before any intervention action is taken.
Data Retention Policy expand_more
Student behavioral data used for AI training is anonymized after each semester. Personal Identifiable Information (PII) is encrypted at rest and in transit, and is never used to train global models.
